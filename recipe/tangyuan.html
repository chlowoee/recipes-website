Tangyuan Recipe

What Am I?

Hiya, I'm Tang Yuan.

I'm a Chinese dessert made of glutinous rice flour in water, containing a sweet black sesame filling.

I'm currently bathing in a subtle ginger sugar syrup <3

This recipe makes 4 servings of me hehe!

What yuu need to make me

AKA Ingredients

  • 1 cup black sesame paste
  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 5cm knob of ginger
  • 1/2 cup warm water

Method

  1. Line a tray with baking paper.
  2. Roll the black sesame filling into balls of diameter 1.5cm
  3. Fill up the tray with these balls
  4. Place tray into the freeze once the tray is filled up
  5. Put the sweet rice flour into a mixing bowl. Slowly add in the warm water, while stirring to form a dough. The dough canâ€™t be too wet, or it will not hold its shape. Cover with a damp towel.
  6. Take the filling tray from out of the freezer
  7. Pinch some dough off and roll until it becomes a sphere
  8. Press the dough ball until flattened into a circle
  9. Place a filling ball onto the dough circle
  10. Wrap up the filling in the dough until it is completely covered
  11. Set ball aside on a tray
  12. Make tangyuan until the tray fills up
  13. Cut the ginger into slices
  14. Bring a medium pot of water to a boil
  15. Drop the ginger into the water
  16. Dissolve the sugar in the water
  17. Bring a large pot of water to a boil
  18. Add the sesame balls and stir immediately to prevent them from sticking. Adding the sesame balls will cool down the water temperature significantly, so once the water comes back up to a boil, add a 1/2 cup of water to the pot.
  19. Take out balls and plate up with a scoop of the ginger syrup
