Mango Smoothie Recipe

What Am I?

Hiya, I'm a Mango Smoothie.

Thirsty? Absolutely parched? Fear not for I am the slayer of dry mouths. I am the hydrator of cracked lips. I shall slay the dreaded dragon of desert-like senses.

What yuu need to make me

AKA Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped ripe mango
  • ½ cup low-fat milk
  • ½ cup ice
  • ¼ cup plain low-fat yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Method

  1. Place the mango, milk, ice, yogurt, and honey in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. Quench that thirst!
