Cat Food Recipe

What Am I?

Hiya, I'm Cat Food.

I'm here to ensure your miau miau stays a happy miau miau

What yuu need to make me

AKA Ingredients

  • 50g raw chicken liver
  • 50g raw beef kidney
  • 2 cups water
  • 100g raw chicken heart
  • 700g raw chicken wings with bones and skin
  • 100g raw salmon with bones
  • 1 tsp taurine supplement powder

Method

  1. Puree all the organ meat
  2. Grind all the ingredients together
  3. Freeze the excess in small portions for easy serving later on
  4. Pet your miau miau
